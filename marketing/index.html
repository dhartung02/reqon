Our philosophy

AI helps. You stay in charge.

Reqon is built to organize and speed up a search you run — not to blast out applications on your behalf. Scoring, drafts, and prep are always reviewable and editable. The goal is quality over quantity, and every decision stays yours.

Reqon helps
Ranks jobs, drafts notes, preps interview answers — all editable before you ever use them.
You decide
You choose what to apply to, what to send, and when. Nothing leaves your hands without your review.
Never auto-applies

Never replaces you
