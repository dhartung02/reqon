The problem

A serious search gets scattered across fifteen surfaces.

Spreadsheets, browser tabs, email threads, saved postings, recruiter DMs, and personal notes. The hard part isn’t finding more roles — it’s keeping the right ones moving without the whole thing going stale.

Spreadsheets Browser tabs Email threads Saved postings Recruiter DMs Sticky notes Calendar reminders
How it works

Discover. Prioritize. Track.

One disciplined loop instead of a dozen open tabs — built to protect your attention, not inflate your activity.

1

Discover

Reqon scouts real ATS boards for roles that match your profile and surfaces them in one place — fresh links, no rotting aggregator noise.

2

Prioritize

Every role is scored for fit and interview probability, then tiered by expected value — so your effort goes to the few that count.

3

Track

A structured pipeline carries each role from lead to offer, with follow-up nudges, interview prep, and recruiter activity in view.

Philosophy

AI assists. You stay in control.

Reqon is a disciplined job-search operating system — not an auto-apply spam tool. Scoring, recommendations, cover notes, and screening answers are always reviewable, editable, and grounded. The goal is quality over quantity: optimize your time for maximum value, and keep every decision yours.

Reqon assists
Scores roles, drafts notes, prepares interview answers — all reviewable and editable before you use them.
You decide
You choose what to apply to, what to send, and when. Nothing leaves your hands without your review.
Reqon never auto-applies
No mass submissions, no bots filling applications on your behalf. It’s built to protect your attention and your accounts.
Reqon never replaces you
It organizes and accelerates a search you run. The judgment — and the credit — stays with you.
