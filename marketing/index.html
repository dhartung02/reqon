The problem

Most tools optimize for volume. Senior candidates need the opposite.

Discovery is noisy and links rot in days. Spreadsheets go stale the moment things get busy. And re-typing the same profile into every ATS burns the effort that should go into the 2–3 applications that actually matter. Reqon gives you a ranked, honest shortlist instead.

⦿
Scout sources for you
Polls Greenhouse, Ashby, Lever and more daily via their real board APIs — no scraping, no rotting aggregator links.
▦
Ranks by real fit
Scores every role for fit and interview probability against your résumé and target criteria, then tiers them by expected value.
◷
Tracks to offer
A pipeline that follows each role from lead to offer, with follow-up nudges, interview prep, and recruiter-reply detection.
How it works

Discover → verify → apply → follow up.

A command center, not a spreadsheet

Open the board and see exactly what needs action today: high-EV roles to apply to, follow-ups gone quiet, interviews to prep, leads to verify — each one a tap from the detail.

Clip anything, fill applications faster

A browser extension clips roles from any posting and an AI apply-assist pre-fills the factual fields from your profile — it never submits, never touches passwords or EEO. You stay in control.

On your phone, in sync

A companion app mirrors your pipeline, today's actions, analytics, and notifications — review and act on the go, fully synced with your board.

Private by design.
