Reqon

Requisition pipeline

Reqon — self-hosted job-search command center. Live requisitions grouped by fit tier, each carrying a fit score, interview-probability estimate, salary, link-confidence, and a full tracking layer (status, dates, recruiter, referral, resume version, follow-ups, next action).

Fit = domain / résumé match. Interview probability = odds of landing a screen, kept separate from fit. Expected value = fit × probability — your apply-effort allocator. Tier is set by expected value.

Every edit saves server-side and persists. Use Run Scout to pull fresh roles from board APIs, and the Today tab for what needs action.

Matching & scoring

Thresholds and keyword lists that decide which roles the scout adds and how it ranks them.

Desired salary

Scores salary fit against the top of each posting's range. At/above target = small fit bonus; at/above the minimum = neutral; below = a fit penalty that scales (well-below roles can drop out). Roles with no posted comp are never penalized. Leave both at 0 to ignore salary. How fit works ↗

Employment types match the role title and are dropped. Blockers demote a match (−2 fit each) so heavy hits fall below your min-fit / min-tier; light hits just rank lower.

AI assistant

Cost control: each run makes at most this many AI calls, and a role isn't re-scored until its posting changes or this many days pass. ~0.5–1k tokens per call on gpt-5.4-mini.

Application draft assistant

Per-req "✍ AI draft" composes a cover note or screening answer from your profile + narratives + JD. Output is editable and never auto-submitted.

Cost estimation

OpenAI can't report your balance via API, so cost is estimated from logged tokens × the rate you set here (was .env-only). Authoritative billing: platform.openai.com/usage ↗

Digest & notifications

The always-on server composes a digest from your data and delivers it to the channels you pick. A file fallback is always written to agent/digest-latest.html.

Pick any combination. A channel that isn't configured yet shows needs setup — set its keys below (Slack/Email/SMS) or under Advanced (iOS push). In-app shows in the board's 🔔 bell.

SMS

Free: sends a text through your carrier's email gateway over the SMTP above (set Email/SMTP first). Carrier-dependent and best-effort — fine for personal alerts. Pick your carrier and enter your mobile number.

Twilio is a paid API. Inert until all four are set. Get the SID + auth token from the Twilio console; From is your Twilio number, To is your mobile.

Test sends to all enabled channels. Save settings before sending. Gmail with 2-Step Verification needs an App Password (Google Account → Security → App passwords), not your account password.

Gmail response ingest (IMAP)

Reads recruiter emails on the server and updates the board — auto-sets rejections, flags interviews/offers for review. Same credential model as the iOS app; this is the server-side equivalent. Needs a Gmail App Password.
